# BBTRS — Client Skill

**Full name**: BioDynamic Breathwork & Trauma Release System
**Founder**: Giten Tonkov
**Website**: biodynamicbreath.com
**Project manager**: Jacque
**Offerings**: Online Practitioner Certification, Advanced Practitioner Certification, individual Giten Tonkov sessions

## Brand Name Rule

Always **"BBTRS"** — all caps. Never "Bbtrs", "bbtrs", or spelled out in copy unless introducing the brand for the first time.

## Brand Colours

| Token | Description | Use |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| Deep forest teal | Dark background | Primary dark bg |
| Warm gold | `approx #c9a84c` | Accent, display highlights |
| Terracotta | Earthy orange-red | Secondary accent |
| Sage green | Muted green | Tertiary accent |
| Cream/parchment | Off-white | Light background |

**Design ethos**: Earthy, spiritual-professional. Not clinical. Not airy-fairy wellness. Grounded in somatic science.

## Typography

- **Display**: Cormorant Garamond (300–400 weight, italic for key phrases)
- **Body**: Raleway (300–400 weight)
- Pair: large Cormorant headings + Raleway body copy = BBTRS signature look

## Copy Voice & Tone

**Voice pillars**: Somatic · Credentialed · Experiential · Deeply human
**POV**: The work speaks for itself. Copy creates space for the reader to feel into it, not convince them.

**Headline approach**: Name the physiological truth before the offer
- ✓ "Trauma isn't stored in the mind. It's held in the body."
- ✗ "Join our world-leading trauma release certification"

**Giten's voice**: Direct, warm, decades of clinical experience. Not academic. Not salesy. Speaks from inside the work.

**CTA language**:
- "Apply Now" (certification pages — applications required)
- "Book a Call" (discovery for high-ticket)
- "Join the Waitlist" (cohort pre-launch)
- Never: "Sign Up", "Buy", "Purchase"

**Prohibited words**: trauma-informed (overused in this space — say "body-centred" or "somatically grounded"), healing journey, powerful, transformational, life-changing

## Copy Rules

- British spelling throughout
- No em dashes
- The 6 elements of BBTRS: always reference in full when introducing the modality
- Certification hours: always state exactly (e.g. "460 training hours")
- Accreditors: always list the specific bodies (confirm current accreditors before publishing)
- Social proof: Giten's years of experience (25+), number of practitioners trained (confirm current figure)

## Design Direction

- "Sacred Editorial" aesthetic — luxury wellness meets Kinfolk magazine
- Light parchment/cream ground for light sections
- Deep forest teal for dark sections
- Gold used surgically — one accent, not decoration
- Enormous Cormorant Garamond at 300 weight for hero headlines
- Organic image shapes (blob border-radius, not hard rectangles)
- Animated breathwork elements (orbs, rings) where appropriate
- No generic wellness stock photography — use BBTRS actual training imagery where available
