## Default Theme: LIGHT

The website primarily uses a **light theme**:
- **Page background**: white `#FFFFFF`
- **Body text**: `#102E2F` (dark teal on white)
- **Section backgrounds**: white or very light
- **Dark sections**: `#102E2F` background with white text — used sparingly for contrast
- **CTA accent**: `#E5BB9C` warm peach-gold on dark or light
- **Rule**: Light background = `#102E2F` text. Dark `#102E2F` bg = white text.

Never default to a dark theme. The brand is clean, airy, and professional with deep teal as the ink colour.

## Typography (extracted from biodynamicbreath.com — verified)

- **Primary font**: **Switzer** (Sans-serif) — clean, modern, slightly geometric. Used for body and UI.
