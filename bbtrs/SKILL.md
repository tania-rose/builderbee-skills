## Copy Voice & Tone

**Voice pillars**: Somatic · Credentialed · Experiential · Deeply human
**POV**: The body holds the answer. Copy creates space for the reader to feel into it, not convince them with logic.

**Headline approach**: Name the physiological truth before the offer
- ✓ "Trauma isn't stored in the mind. It's held in the body."
- ✗ "Join our world-leading trauma release certification"

**Giten's voice**: Direct, warm, deeply experienced. Not academic. Not salesy. Speaks from inside the work. The FEEL TO HEAL philosophy underpins everything.

**CTA language**:
- "Apply Now" (certification pages — applications required)
- "Book a Call" (discovery for high-ticket)
- "Join the Waitlist" (cohort pre-launch)
- "Start Your Journey" (used on website — acceptable)
- Never: "Sign Up", "Buy", "Purchase"

**Prohibited words**: healing journey, powerful transformation, life-changing, trauma-informed (overused — say "body-centred" or "somatically grounded" instead), unlock your potential
