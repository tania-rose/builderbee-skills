# BuilderBee Universal Standards

Read this BEFORE any client-specific skill. These rules apply to every piece of work produced by BuilderBee, regardless of client.

## Copy Rules (non-negotiable)

- **No em dashes** anywhere. Replace with a comma, colon, or rewrite the sentence. This is a hard rule — the character — is banned in all copy.
- **No AI giveaways**: Never use words like "delve", "unlock", "unleash", "elevate", "game-changer", "leverage", "dive into", "it's worth noting", "certainly", "absolutely".
- **No passive voice** in headlines or CTAs. Always active and direct.
- **No bullet point padding**: If it can be said in prose, say it in prose.
- **Specificity over vagueness**: "14-day guarantee" not "flexible guarantee". "6 modules over 6 weeks" not "comprehensive curriculum".
- **Outcome-led copy**: Lead with what the client's client gets, not what the product does.
- **No exclamation marks** in body copy. One maximum per page in CTAs only.

## Design Rules

- **Dark background = light text always**. Never dark text on dark backgrounds.
- **No generic AI aesthetics**: No Inter/Roboto/Arial fonts, no purple gradients on white, no cookie-cutter layouts.
- **Mobile-first**: Every page must be checked at 375px width mentally.
- **Brand colours only**: Never introduce colours not in the client's palette.
- **Wave dividers over hard edges** for organic/wellness brands.
