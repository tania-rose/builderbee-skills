# Alchemy of Breath (AOB) — Client Skill

**Contacts**: Anthony Abbagnano (founder), Amy Rachelle (operations)
**Website**: alchemyofbreath.com
**Primary offers**: The Calm Code certification, Facilitator Training, BreathCamps, ASHA Tuscany retreat

## Brand Identity

AOB sits at the intersection of clinical breathwork science and conscious education. The tone is warm, grounded, and credentialed — not mystical or airy-fairy. Anthony's voice is direct, knowledgeable, and deeply human.

## Brand Colours (apply to ALL AOB digital assets)

| Token | Hex | Use |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Gold | `#E1B668` | Primary accent, display text highlight |
| Gold 2 | `#D6A652` | Hover states, secondary gold |
| Teal | `#00a5b2` | Digital CTA buttons, links, labels |
| Dark Teal | `#5b7889` | Secondary digital colour |

| Black | `#000000` | Sparingly |
| White | `#ffffff` | Light backgrounds, reversed text |

**Light bg**: use `#F7F4EF` warm off-white or white — never grey.
**Rule**: Dark background = light/white text. Light background = ink text. No exceptions.

## Typography

- **Digital display**: Cormorant Garamond (300–400 weight, italic for emotional hook words)
- **Digital body**: Khula (300–600 weight)
- **Print**: Gotham
- **No other fonts** unless explicitly approved

## Brand Name Rules

- Always written as **"alchemy of breath"** — fully lowercase in running copy
- Acronym **AOB** acceptable in internal/team comms only
- Never "Alchemy Of Breath" (capital O) in copy

## Copy Voice & Tone

**Voice pillars**: Grounded · Credentialed · Human · Precise
**POV**: The reader already senses something is calling them. We name that clearly rather than trying to convince them.

**Headline approach**: Lead with the nervous system truth, not the product feature.
- ✓ "Your body already knows how to regulate. We teach you to read it."
- ✗ "Join our world-class breathwork certification programme"

**CTA language**:
- "Secure My Place" (enrolment pages)
- "Join the Waitlist" (pre-launch)
- "Book Your Clarity Call" (discovery)
- Never: "Sign Up", "Register", "Buy Now"

**Prohibited words for AOB**: heal, journey, transformation, powerful, game-changing, unlock your potential, awaken

**Tagline (Calm Code)**: "Read the pattern. Apply the breath. Regulate the nervous system."

## Copy Rules

- British spelling throughout (programme not program, recognise not recognize)
- No em dashes anywhere — use commas, colons, or rewrite
- Italics in Cormorant Garamond for the emotional/poetic word in a headline
- Testimonials must be specific and outcome-led — no vague praise
- Social proof: "2,500+ certified facilitators worldwide" (not 1,000+)
